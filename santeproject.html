Sante

Sante is an inclusive plant based recipe app that is here to help change the way people cook in the kitchen.

Problem

What's missing in the recipe market space?

Sante is designed to be a fun and simple plant based recipe site that helps people change their mindset on cooking. Plant based eating doesn’t need to feel scary or complicated or just a salad and Sante’s here to make that even easier.

For those who have not or will not fully convert to a plant based diet but want to reduce their impact on the environment, improve their health or they’re hosting a family gathering with a variety of dietary restrictions, Sante’s here to help make plant based eating less foreign. This site isn’t about being healthy or converting people, it’s just to help people try new things and have fun doing it. Plant based eating isn’t just salads and Sante will showcase some fierce blueberry pancakes you can make and help you find the ingredients or use what you have in your pantry!

There are tons of people who follow various vegan lifestyles. Finding a place that serves all vegans lacks in the market. The junkfood vegan who wants a burger or the raw foods vegan who wants to try making zoodles for dinner….these individuals can all find their needs on Sante. In addition, vegans always feel ‘on alert’ for finding out if something may not be truly vegan, but Sante will become their safe place to find truly 100% vegan recipes.

Solution

  • Simplicity : Responsive Web App to capture more users
  • Safe Place with options : Sorts and Filters

With a responsive app you can use Sante on any device. Sante makes every step of cooking easy. Sign into your account on your phone and use your Sante shopping like at the grocery store, use your tablet at home to cook in the kitchen and for the old school way, use your desktop to print. Sante wants you to be you and is prepared to adapt to your cooking habits.

Select a recipe and view all you need to follow through to a great meal! Step-by-step instructions, videos and more! Play with serving sizes to customize your recipe for your family and friends.

How were the solutions identified?

User research was conducted to allow me to come up with these solutions and opportunities in the plant based recipe market.

Gathering User Requirements

In order to begin developing the foundation to a responsive web plant based app, I needed to gather information from future users to ensure all their needs were captured.

Hypothesis

We believe that by offering a variety of easy, plant-based recipes for users who want to feel less intimidated by plant based cooking or build trust (a truly vegan recipe site) our users will keep returning.

No matter the reason for the user using Sante, they will always feel included but not pressured to be someone they are not. Our users want to change and no matter the reason we want to be there to support them.

Success Metrics

In order to measure success, we will be tracking our sign-ups and user behaviors.

Quantitative

  • Number of Profiles Created
  • Number of Email Signups
  • Number of Subscriptions
  • Most-used Search Options

Qualitative

  • Submitted user feedback and reviews
  • User reviews and likes for recipes

MVP Rationale

One of the biggest challenges transitioning to a plant-based diet is most people feel they have not learned how to cook this way. It can seem daunting and overwhelming to change the way you view food and nutrition. This app is focused on building confidence and a community of people learning new ways of thinking and eating. Sante has prioritized the below MVP’s :

  1. Recipe Search
  2. Nutrition
  3. Time

User Persona

Design Process

User Flows Diagrams: Creating user stories.

  • As a cook. I want to find a recipe easily, so I can save time
  • As a cook, I want to find recipes specific to my diet, so that I don’t waste time researching
  • As a healthy individual, I want a recipe and it’s nutritional details, so I can continue to feel healthy
  • As a healthy individual, I want to reduce animal product from my diet, so I can reduce my chances of heart disease, which runs in my family
  • As a busy office worker, I want to be able to save and organize recipes, so I can recreate my favorite meals
